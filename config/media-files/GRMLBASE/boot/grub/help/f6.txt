Grml - Linux for sysadmins and users of texttools

 Troubleshooting on Grml (main page via Help menu)

  If you experience lockups or other hardware failures,
  disable buggy APIC interrupt routing:

            grml noapic nolapic

  Disable ACPI for PCI maps (some HP servers and Via-based machines):

            grml pci=noacpi

  Deactivate SMP-kernel and use only one processor:

            grml nosmp    /   grml maxcpus=1

  Disable framebuffer if you get a black/blank screen or
  notice problems with your display:

            grml vga=normal
